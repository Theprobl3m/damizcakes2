Little Story

Hi! I am Dami
Founder and Creative Director at Dami'z Cakes

Our story is nothing short of sweet because every cake has a story to tell.
My journey began in 2017 when I used my last savings to purchase a 6-piece cupcake baking pan, some flour, butter, sugar,eggs, baking powder, flavour and cupcake wraps to try baking cupcakes using recipes I learnt from Youtube. From the first 6 cupcakes to the first birthday cake,I have created beautiful cakes over the years that beat your imagination.
Shuffling school and business between two states has been a real struggle, but I utilize every single moment I have to making sure I pay attention to detail in every cake making and designing process to produce the perfect cake with love.
